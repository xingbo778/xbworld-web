<h1>XBWorld Manual</h1>
<img src='/static/images/frontpage-jumbotron.png' style="max-width: 40%;"><br><br>

XBWorld is an AI-powered civilization strategy game inspired by the
history of human civilization. The game commences in prehistory and your mission
is to lead your tribe from the Stone Age to the Space Age...<br><br>

XBWorld is free and open source software, released under the <a href="https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.html" target='_new'>
GNU Affero General Public License</a>. The Freeciv C server is released under the
<a href="https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-2.0.html" target='_new'>GNU General
Public License</a>. <br>

XBWorld is based on <a href='https://github.com/freeciv/freeciv-web' target='_new'>Freeciv-web</a>,
originally created by <a href='http://www.twitter.com/andreasrosdal' target='_new'>
Andreas R&oslash;sdal</a>. See the full
<a href='https://www.freeciv.org/wiki/People' target='_new'>credits for Freeciv</a>.
